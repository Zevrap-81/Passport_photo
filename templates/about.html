About

Welcome to the Passport Photo Maker, an AI-powered tool that helps you create high-quality passport photos.

Our app utilizes advanced algorithms to analyze and process your uploaded photos, ensuring they meet the specifications required for passport photos.

More concretely we use algorithms such as U2Net for removing background and Google's Mediapipe for tracking facial orientation

Key Features:

  • Upload your photo and receive a processed passport photo in seconds
  • Automatic cropping and adjustment to meet official passport photo guidelines
  • Download your processed photo and print it directly or save it for online submissions
  • Simple and intuitive user interface for easy photo processing

Whether you need a passport photo for travel, identification, or any other purpose, our app makes it quick and convenient to generate compliant photos from the comfort of your own home.

Thank you for using the Passport Photo App. If you have any questions or feedback, please don't hesitate to contact us.
